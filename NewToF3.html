So, you got past our favorite excuses and decided to participate? Greatness.

We do not show up at F3 for ourselves. We show up for the man standing beside each of us.

Here is one undeniable truth that I know about each of us, we need authentic relationships with other men who are working to be strong fathers, husbands and leaders. Isolation will destroy a man! We have the opportunity to change our community and the trajectory of our families.  Getting in the best shape of your life is just a by product. The work out is the magnet, the relationships and community with other men will keep bringing you back. Lock shields with us and help us accomplish F3’s mission to invigorate male leadership in our community.

  1.  When you are first starting, it is important to remember to MODIFY as needed during the beatdowns. If you want to go harder – go harder. If you have not exercised in a long time, slow down. Don’t hurt yourself or even worse, get discouraged. Just show up, work hard and give it your best. That is all that matters. We are not here competing against one another. We are freaking proud of you for showing up at 5:30 in the morning to exercise!
  2. Be sure to check out the About Us.
  3. Read our disclaimer, then simply pick a place to post.
  4. Visit our Locations to see what site works best for you.
  5. Check out the Lexicon. We have a nick name for everything you do. The Lexicon will give you a definition behind our secret language.
  6. All you need to bring is an open mind and a positive attitude. Wear your regular outdoor workout clothes and, unless you have farmer hands, consider bringing a pair of gloves.
  7. If you are not meeting a friend at your first post, simply show up and introduce yourself as an FNG. You’ll be welcomed.
  8. Sign up for twitter. Follow @f3nation. Follow your local region. Follow your fellow PAX.
  9. If you’re someone who’s interested in service, check out and attend an F3 2nd F event.
  10. Ask us any questions we didn’t cover.

Welcome, Friendly New Guy!

Congratulations on tackling your first F3 workout with such success! The first one is always the hardest–they don’t get easier, you just get stronger.

There’s a lot to catch you up on regarding F3 and what we’re doing here in North Austin, Texas! To help you get up to speed quickly, we’ve come up with a list of the Top 5 Things To Do After Your First F3 Post:

1. Join us on Slack

We do most of our communicating on Slack and, now that you’re an official member of F3 NATX, we’d like to loop you in to our communications. The number of F3 NATX Slack channels you can join can get confusing, so we recommend starting off with these Slack channels: #natx-news, #natx-comz, #mettleofhonor, #thegreyhound, #thebighouse, and #robins-hood.

Join our Slack workspace.

2. Come back for another workout

Like we said before, your first F3 workout is always the hardest, but the workouts never really seem to get easier… you just get stronger the longer you keep at it! We currently have workouts every Monday, Wednesday, and Friday from 5:00am – 5:45am, and workouts every Saturday from 7:00am – 8:00am.

Here’s the F3 NATX Workout Schedule.

3. Explore the F3 NATX website

Since you’re here, you can already start to check this one off your list! But, don’t stop with our Friendly New Guy welcome page… there’s a lot of great information here:

  • About F3
  • Backblasts
  • Q Sheet
  • Events

4. Sign up for the NATX Newsletter

We’ve set up a mailing list to keep everyone up-to-date on major F3 NATX news and updates: social events, volunteer opportunities, new workout times/locations, etc. If you’re posting at workouts and active on our Slack workspace, you’ll probably already know about most of these updates, but the NATX Newsletter is another great way to stay in the loop.

Join our NATX Newsletter mailing list.

5. Find out more about F3 Nation

If you don’t know already, F3 is a nationwide network of free, peer-led workout groups for guys of all ages and fitness levels. There are F3 workouts held almost every day across the nation and in the great State of Texas.

We like to give just about everything its very own F3 nickname, whether it be an exercise (Merkins), an F3 member (PAX), or even inanimate objects (Fartsack). Your head is probably swirling with all the crazy nicknames you heard during your first workout, but don’t worry–you will soon catch on! To help you out, F3 Nation has created a Lexicon to help you with all of the non-exercise-related nicknames and an Exicon to help you with all of the exercise-related nicknames.

Check out F3 Nation for even more information.

You should be all set! We are so happy to welcome you to F3 NATX and can’t wait to SYITG (See You In The Gloom) again very soon!

Aye!
