Fitness, Fellowship and Faith

F3 is a national network of free, peer-led workouts for men of all ages and fitness levels. Our mission is to plant, grow and serve small workout groups for men for the invigoration of male community leadership.

New To F3
F3 in Austin, TX

Established in 2017, our growing free men’s workout group has expanded to many workouts throughout the week, monthly happy hours and coffeeteria, and volunteer work to help our greater Austin community
